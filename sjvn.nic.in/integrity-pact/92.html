Integrity Pact Programme

Implementation of Integrity Pact for Contracts of SJVN Limited

A Memorandum of Understanding for implementation of Integrity Pact in SJVN and its subsidiaries has been signed between SJVN and Transparency International India (TII) on 01.06.2018.

The Integrity Pact is applicable for procurement having estimated cost above the threshold limits as under:-

  • For Procurement of Goods the threshold limit is Rs. 8 Lacs
  • For Procurement of Services the threshold limit is Rs. 15 Lacs
  • For Procurement of Works the threshold limit is Rs. 50 Lacs

To oversee the implementation of Integrity Pact, Sh. Shitala Prasad Shrivastava and Smt. Archana Pandey Tiwari, have been appointed as Independent External Monitors (IEM) with the consent of Central Vigilance Commission (CVC).

The relevant documents for implementation of Integrity Pact are as under:

  • Memorandum of Understanding signed between SJVN and TII

  • Contact details of Independent External Monitors (IEMs)

  • Format of Integrity Pact to be signed between Contractor and SJVN
