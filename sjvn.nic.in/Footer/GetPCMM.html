SJVN is proud to announce that its Talent Management practices have been successfully assessed to the CMMI institute's People Capability Maturity Model at Maturity Level 3. SJVN is the first PSU to be awarded Level-3 by CMMI Institute.

PCMM (short names: People CMM, PCMM, P-CMM) is a global best practice framework for people practices. It is a maturity framework that focuses on continuously improving the management and development of the human assets of an organization. SJVN started its journey for PCMM in 2018 and achieved Level-2 in April, 2019 and after continuous efforts has now achieved Level-3.

This assessment is a testimony to SJVN’s commitment to excellence in HR practices and its belief in its human capital capability as a key lever to the realization of its Shared Vision.
